Quotes Help To Stay Focused On Your Life Goals
We are surrounded by information; some are great, but most is distractions that separate us from our goals. Quotes are one of the tools you can use to regain the focus you need to accomplish your life goals. They are short and precise, you don't spend too much time reading them, and they are effective. Quotes can be your access to the minds of the wisest people who ever lived.

Alright, coders, your mission is to create a React app that generates quotes to stay focused, grab that knowledge, and make your own conclusions.

You'll be using a simple API (https://talaikis.com/api/quotes/random/). Every time you call, it will generate a new quote, with no key required.
